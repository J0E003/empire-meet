After you install (one-time setup, just like Zoom)

  1. Open Empire Meet and join or start a meeting.
  2. macOS: the first time control is used, grant Accessibility + Screen Recording when macOS asks (System Settings › Privacy & Security). Windows: nothing to grant.
  3. Share your screen → approve the control request → the other person can really click and type on your machine. Revoke anytime.

Unsigned build: on first open, right-click the app → Open (macOS) or “More info → Run anyway” (Windows SmartScreen).
